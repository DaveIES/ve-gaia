CO2 & Energy

The results in this report are generated by the IES ApacheSim module. ApacheSim is a rigorous building thermal simulation approach that conforms to ANSI/ASHRAE Standard 140. If you wish to know more about ApacheSim and obtain a copy of the ANSI/ASHRAE 140 standard report then visit iesve.com

Details notes:

  • PPD is the Predicted Percentage Dissatisfied. This value represents the percentage of people that will find the room thermally uncomfortable.
  • ASHRAE 55 states comfort lies between 5 and 10% PPD (US Only)
